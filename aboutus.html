THE BAKER

Born in 1992, studied ART at school and ever since continued to practice her love for art. A self-taught baker which implements her love for creativity in what she creates.

Woodenspoons & Whisks offers a variety of cake flavours as well as the right combination of frosting; from the simplest taste of chocolate ganache to the delicate taste of Italian meringue buttercream. There are various flavours one can choose from: Vanilla, Chocolate, Red Velvet, Carrot, White Chocolate, Citrus flavours and much more. Although the customer has the availability to choose which frosting likes best, the baker will help to choose the best combination for the most delightful, mouth-watering piece of cake.

ABOUT THE CAKES

Every cake is made and baked using high quality local products and no premixes are used. Love and passion is a must when it comes to desserts which is reflected in the most heavenly cakes one ever tasted.

THE BAKER will have a consultation with the clients and will help them to choose the best design for the cake in relation to their special occassion. Once the design is finalised the process starts right away to provide the clients with the best presentation of the requested cake.
