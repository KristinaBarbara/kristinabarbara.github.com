THE BAKER

Woodenspoons & Whisks officially commenced in mid 2017.

Born in 1992, studied ART at school and ever since continued to practice her love for art. A self-taught baker which implements her love for creativity in what she creates.

Woodenspoons & Whisks offers a variety of cake flavours as well as the right combination of frosting; from the simplest taste of chocolate ganache to the delicate taste of Italian meringue buttercream
